Schedule & Contents

Today Schedule

from 13:00 - 14:00 hours

1.Share Experience and tracked values

During this part of the session students will share with us the experience of the weekend, pictures of the plants and the values.

from 14:00 - 15:30 Hours

2. Ideation and Brainstorming

During this part of the session, students will be divided by teams. Each team should be able to ideate and create several proposals related to communicate with plants. Brainstorming, discussions and another techniques should be use in order to share ideas.

from 15:30 - 17:00 Hours

3.  Conceptualize

During this part of the sessions students students should put the ideas into more realistic scenarios. Take the best ideas and propose some experimental approaches, commercial services, products, educational experiences etc.

Cape program is an educational program for students from different backgrounds and nationalities, including those coming from companies and local governments,and through education, it intends to promote the "contribution of plants to the environment" in a range of urban environments.
